About US

The Addis Ababa Institute of Technology (AAiT) is the engineering and technology wing of Addis Ababa University. AAiT, led by an Executive Director with the rank of Vice President, currently has six major Schools led by Deans, namely, School of Civil and Environmental Engineering (SCEE), School of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering (SMiE), School of Electrical and Computer Engineering (SECE), School of Chemical and Bio Engineering (SCBE), School of Information Technology and Engineering (SiTE), and School of Multidisciplinary Engineering (SMDE) which hosts the Centers for Bio-Medical Engineering, Railway Engineering, Materials Engineering, and Renewable Energy Technology. In addition to offering a wide range of academic programs at undergraduate and graduate (MSc & PhD) levels, the schools and centers are actively engaged in various problem solving research, community engagement and professional consultancy services.
